👋 About Me

Prior to joining HKUST, I obtained my B.S. in Computer Science (Advanced Program) from University of Science, VNU-HCM. I graduated with highest distinction, achieving a GPA of 3.89/4.0. My research focuses on Vision-Language Models, Multimodal Agents, and Reasoning AI on with a core emphasis on advancing image/video understanding and vision-action world modeling to enable agents that can perceive, reason, and act in complex environments.
